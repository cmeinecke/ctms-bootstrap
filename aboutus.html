About Us

Orchestra at CTMS

Chisholm Trail Middle School is a 2C middle school in the Round Rock Independent School District. Colin Meinecke joined as the director in the 2019-2020 school year. CTMS orchestras consistently place many students in the Region XXVI Orchestras and receive Sweepstakes awards for the two groups that attend the UIL Concert & Sight-Reading Evaluation. CTMS Orchestra has many opportunities for performing, with curricular concerts as well as community performances.

About the Orchestras

  • First-Year Orchestra
  • Philharmonic Orchestra
  • Honors Orchestra

Students in First-Year Orchestra learn instrument and music fundamentals in classes divided by instrument (violin, viola, cello, bass, and harp). No prior experience is needed to join!

Philharmonic Orchestra students continue working on their music skills in a mixed orchestra class.

Honors Orchestra is the most advanced string ensemble at CTMS. Students have the opportunity to play more advanced music and audition for All-Region Orchestra.

About the Director

Colin Meinecke has been the director at CTMS since 2019. Prior to this position, he was orchestra director at Alamo Heights High School in San Antonio. He holds degrees in Music Performance from the University of North Texas and Yale University.
